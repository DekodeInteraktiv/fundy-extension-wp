== Description ==

Your donation form is the heartbeat of fundraising on your website — so why settle for solutions that don’t perform?

[Dekode Fundraising](https://intro.fundy.cloud) puts the donor experience first.

=== Dekode Fundraising is fast ===
Set up a donation form in under a minute, no developers needed.

=== Seamless payment & CRM integrations ===
Works with Vipps, Nets, AvtaleGiro and more, connects to ProFundo, SalesForce and Dynamics.

=== Dekode Fundraising converts ===
Built on years of experience in nonprofit fundraising, Dekode Fundraising removes friction and makes your content shine.

== External services ==

This plugin connects to an API to facilitate the fundraising functionality, both to show forms and to handle submissions.

It sends users personal data (manually entered into a form) when they submit a form.

This service is provided by "Dekode Fundraising": [privacy policy](https://fundy.cloud/en/page/privacy).

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Do I need a Dekode Fundraising account? =
