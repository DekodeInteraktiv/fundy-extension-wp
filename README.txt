Fundy is the fundraising engine that actually converts

== Description ==

Your donation form is the heartbeat of fundraising on your website — so why settle for solutions that don’t perform?

[Fundy](https://intro.fundy.cloud) puts the donor experience first.

=== Fundy is fast ===
Set up a donation form in under a minute, no developers needed.

=== Seamless payment & CRM integrations ===
Works with Vipps, Nets, AvtaleGiro and more, connects to ProFundo, SalesForce and Dynamics.

=== Fundy converts ===
Built on years of experience in nonprofit fundraising, Fundy removes friction and makes your content shine.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Do I need a Fundy account? =

Yes, this plugin requires a [Fundy](https://intro.fundy.cloud) subscription to use.
